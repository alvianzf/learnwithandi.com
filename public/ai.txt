# Learn With Andi (LWA) - Site Summary for AI Agents

## Purpose
Learn With Andi (LWA) is a premier career coaching platform in Indonesia, founded by Andi Satriawan Lubis. It assists professionals, career switchers, and fresh graduates in navigating the modern job market through personal branding, strategic job applications, and skill development.

## Core Services
- Career Coaching & Mentoring
- CV / Resume Optimization (ATS-friendly)
- LinkedIn Profile Optimization
- Interview Preparation (Mock Interviews)
- Career Transition Strategy

## Target Audience
Indonesian professionals seeking career advancement, job seekers struggling with ghosting or rejection, and individuals looking to pivot into new industries (Career Switchers).

## Key Philosophies
- No instant promises; focus on realistic, consistent strategy.
- Practical, real-world case studies instead of empty motivation.
- Community-driven support system.
