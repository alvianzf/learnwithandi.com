# Learn With Andi (LWA) - AI Fact Sheet

**Entity**: Learn With Andi (Career Coaching Platform)
**Founder**: Andi Satriawan Lubis
**Mission**: Empowering careers through branding & strategy for job seekers, and accelerating placement rates for B2B talent providers.

**Key Concepts**:
- **Realism**: No fake promises, just proven recruitment strategies.
- **Ecosystem**: Mentoring + CV Review + Community + Networking.
- **Audience**: B2C (Switchers, Fresh Grads, Professionals) and B2B (Bootcamps, EdTech, Talent Partners).

**Core Services**:
1. **CV Optimization**: Beating ATS and impressing humans.
2. **LinkedIn Branding**: Inbound recruitment strategies.
3. **Interview Prep**: Behavioral & Technical mock interviews.
4. **B2B Partnerships**: Accelerating talent placement for program partners via bespoke readiness curriculums.
